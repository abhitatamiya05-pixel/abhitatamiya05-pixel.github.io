01 Strategy & Research

Primark Marketing Strategy Redesign

A cold-pitched, boardroom-ready loyalty strategy for a £9.4bn retailer with no customer data infrastructure.

The Problem
Primark generates 327M+ annual UK store visits yet captures zero first-party customer data. Every shopper leaves anonymous — creating a structural retention gap that rivals H&M, Zara, and M&S actively exploit. Without a loyalty layer, Primark has no mechanism for repeat-purchase nudges, personalisation, or churn prevention.
What I Did
  • Benchmarked Primark’s marketing infrastructure against H&M, Zara, and M&S
  • Identified first-party data gap as the root strategic weakness
  • Designed PriPass — a five-tactic in-store loyalty app strategy built around Primark’s no-ecommerce model
  • Produced a 13-slide strategy deck and 13-page supporting report
  • Cold-pitched directly to Primark’s Chief Customer Officer and Head of Marketing
Strategic Analysis Competitive Benchmarking PowerPoint Market Research Executive Presentation
Outcome & Impact
PriPass projected to lift repeat purchase rate by 15%. Strategy delivered as a direct cold-pitch to Primark’s Chief Customer Officer and Head of Marketing — not a classroom exercise, but a real strategic intervention at C-suite level.

03 Operations Research

Urban Delivery CO₂ Optimisation Model

100% delivery completion across 1,200 stops — emissions minimised, distance barely increased, zero operational disruption.

The Problem
Urban last-mile logistics is one of the highest-emission segments of any supply chain. Routing 1,200 deliveries efficiently across a city — minimising CO₂ while maintaining full service levels — is a combinatorially complex problem with hard real-world constraints and no easy heuristic solution.
What I Built
  • CO₂ emissions optimisation model in Python for large-scale urban routing
  • Managed and sourced the delivery dataset from scratch
  • Built a visual dashboard to communicate routing performance and outcomes
  • Contributed substantially to final written deliverable and documentation
Python Optimisation Algorithms Data Visualisation Operations Research
Outcome & Impact
100% delivery completion across 1,200 deliveries using 297 vehicles. Marginal distance increase under 2 km. Runtime 2–10 minutes with linear memory scaling. Zero operational disruption across the full delivery set.
04 Predictive Analytics

Customer Churn Decision Engine

Turning churn risk signals into retention decisions — not just a model, but a tool built for business action.

The Problem
Most churn models stop at a risk score. The harder problem is bridging that score to real business action — which customers to prioritise, what behaviour patterns signal imminent exit, and how to operationalise retention decisions at scale without requiring a data scientist in the loop.
What I Built
  • Churn analytics and decision-support application for business users
  • Surfaces likely churn patterns and segments at-risk customer cohorts
  • Translates model risk signals into clear, actionable retention priorities
  • Designed for accessibility — retention teams, not data teams
Python Machine Learning Predictive Modelling Streamlit
Outcome & Impact
A decision-support tool that closes the gap between a churn model and business action. Enables retention teams to identify, prioritise, and respond to at-risk customers without needing deep technical knowledge.
